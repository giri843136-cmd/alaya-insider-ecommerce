## 2. SEO Master Plan

### 2.1 Strategic Approach

Google's 2026 algorithm prioritizes **E-E-A-T** (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness). For affiliate sites specifically:

- **Experience** is the #1 differentiator — first-hand product photos, personal usage stories, honest pros/cons
- **Thin affiliation** (copying manufacturer descriptions) is penalized
- **Investigative intent** keywords (comparisons, "best for X", "vs", "worth it") outperform generic "best X" lists
- **Original research** and unique data builds authority

## 3. Affiliate Strategy

### 3.1 Commission Optimization

ALAYA INSIDER uses a **multi-network + direct merchant** hybrid strategy to maximize commission:

|---------|-----------------|-----------------|----------|-------------------|

| **ShareASale** | 5%-30% | 30 days | Niche brands, Digital | ✅ Configured |

### 3.2 Amazon Associates Strategy

Despite 24-hour cookies and lower rates, Amazon is critical because:

1. **Highest conversion rate** — Prime members trust the checkout
2. **Vast catalog** — Every product we review is available on Amazon
3. **Global reach** — Amazon sites in US, UK, DE, FR, IT, ES, JP, IN, CA, AU

**Amazon Optimization Tactics:**

| Tactic | Implementation | Expected Impact |
|--------|---------------|-----------------|
| **Deep linking** | Link directly to product pages, not search results | +15% conversion |
| **Tracking ID** | Use `alayainsider-21` consistently | Baseline tracking |
| **Tagged URLs** | Add `?tag=alayainsider-21` to all links | Consistent attribution |
| **Product badges** | "Check Price on Amazon" with disclosure | +5% CTR |
| **Price comparison** | Show Amazon price vs. competitors | +10% trust |
| **Prime badge** | Highlight Prime-eligible products | +20% conversion |

## 5. Editorial Strategy

### 5.1 Content Types & Purpose

| Type | Purpose | Conversion Rate | SEO Value | Monthly Target |
|------|---------|----------------|-----------|---------------|
| **Buying Guide** | Long-tail commercial intent | High | Very High | 4-6 |
| **Product Review** | Specific product search | Very High | High | 4-6 |
| **Comparison** | Purchase decision | Very High | Medium | 2-3 |
| **Best List** | Broad commercial intent | Medium | Very High | 2-3 |
| **How-To Guide** | Informational → Commercial | Low | Very High | 2 |
| **Gift Guide** | Seasonal commercial | High | Medium | 2 (seasonal) |
| **Brand Spotlight** | Brand authority | Medium | Medium | 1 |
| **Editorial/Story** | Brand building | Low | Medium | 1 |

### 5.2 Content Standards

#### Buying Guide Standards
- **Minimum 1,200 words** (1,500-2,000 preferred)
- **Featured image** + product images for each recommendation
- **5-10 recommended products** with prices and rationales
- **Personal experience evidence** — photos, anecdotes, testing notes
- **Comparison table** for quick reference
- **"What to look for" checklist**
- **Affiliate disclosure** at the top
- **Expert author byline** with credentials
- **Internal links** to 3 related articles
- **Schema**: Article + Product rich results

#### Review Standards
- **Minimum 800 words** per review
- **First-hand experience** with original photos
- **Pros/Cons** clearly listed
- **Rating** (1-5 scale) with breakdown
- **Compared to** 2 alternatives
- **Best for** recommendations
- **Price comparison** across merchants
- **Buying options** with current pricing
- **Affiliate disclosure** before CTA

#### Comparison Standards
- **2-5 products** compared
- **Feature comparison table**
- **Price comparison** across merchants
- **Verdict** for different use cases
- **Original photos** of all products if possible
- **Side-by-side rating**

### 5.3 AI Usage Policy

| Use | Permitted | Requires Disclosure |
|-----|-----------|-------------------|
| Research assistance | ✅ Yes | ❌ No |
| Outline generation | ✅ Yes | ❌ No |
| Draft generation | ⚠️ With heavy editing | ✅ Yes |
| Full AI-written | ❌ No | N/A |
| Product descriptions | ❌ No (Google penalty risk) | N/A |
| Meta descriptions | ✅ Yes | ❌ No |
| Editing/Proofreading | ✅ Yes | ❌ No |
| Image generation | ⚠️ Must be labeled | ✅ Yes |

**Rule:** All content must have significant human input. AI-generated content that is not substantially edited will violate Google's content policies and risks de-ranking. Every article must include:
- First-hand experience evidence
- Original photos or screenshots
- Personal anecdotes or observations
- Unique analysis or data

### 5.4 Fact-Checking & Product Testing

| Content Type | Fact-Check Required | Testing Required |
|-------------|-------------------|-----------------|
| Buying Guide | ✅ Price, specs, availability | ✅ At least 3 products tested |
| Product Review | ✅ All claims verified | ✅ Product physically tested |
| Comparison | ✅ All specs accurate | ✅ All products tested |
| News/Deals | ✅ Price + availability fresh | ❌ N/A |
| How-To | ✅ All steps verified | ✅ Method tested |

### 5.5 Author Guidelines

- Each article must have a **named author** with a bio
- Author bio includes: name, role, expertise, years of experience
- Author profiles linked from every article
- Multiple authors differentiated by vertical expertise
- No anonymous or pseudonymous content for reviews

### 5.6 Image Guidelines

| Requirement | Standard |
|-------------|----------|
| Hero image | 1200×630px minimum (OG standard) |
| Product images | 800×800px square, white/neutral background |
| Original photos | 1200px+ wide, well-lit, clean composition |
| Alt text | Descriptive, includes product name + keyword |
| File format | WebP preferred, JPEG fallback |
| File size | <200KB per image |
| Credits | All non-original images credited |
| Brand usage | Respect trademarks, no logo modification |
